The 'super' keyword
---------------------
--> super is used to call a super class constructor.
 
-->we Specify  the word super along with the required arguments in the constructor of the derived class
 
--> super() will  make a call to the constructor in the base class. 

The following  example makes use of the keyword super.


super can be used as follows

-->for calling the methods of a super class.
-->for calling the member variables of a super class.
-->for invoking  constructors of super class.
